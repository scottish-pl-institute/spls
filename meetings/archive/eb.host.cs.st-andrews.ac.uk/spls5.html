SPLS Meeting: Wednesday 1st February 2006

Introduction

The February 2006 SPLS meeting is the fifth in the Scottish Programming Languages Seminar series. The meetings are open and interested participants are encouraged to attend.

Location

Room 0.35 (Second Year Lab),
Jack Cole Building,
School of Computer Science,
University of St Andrews,
North Haugh,
St Andrews,
Fife.

Details of how to find the building can be found here. There is also a map.
