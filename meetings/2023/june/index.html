Scottish Programming Languages Seminar

Wednesday, 7th June 2023

The Scottish Programming Languages Seminar (SPLS) is an informal meeting for discussing anything related to programming languages.

This edition of SPLS will be held both in-person and online. The in-person meeting will take place in the Coats Building, room P118 at the University of the West of Scotland.

Attending

Registration

  • Please register by 30th May on the SPLS Doodle poll if you plan to attend SPLS in person.
  • Registration is required for catering. There are no COVID restrictions on attendance. Mask wearing in packed indoor areas is welcomed, as is testing before attending.

Travel

  • By train from Glasgow Central to Paisley Gilmour Street and a 10 minute walk via High Street.
  • Glasgow Central Train Station is a 10 minute walk from Glasgow Queen Street Train Station.
  • Car parking is available at the nearby Paisley Centre Car Park.
