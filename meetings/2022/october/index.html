Scottish Programming Languages Seminar

Wednesday, 26th October 2022

The Scottish Programming Languages Seminar (SPLS) is an informal meeting for discussing anything related to programming languages.

This edition of SPLS will be held both in-person and online. The in-person meeting will take place in the Cottrell Building at the Unversity of Stirling.
