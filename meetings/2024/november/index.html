SPLS

The Scottish Programming Languages Seminar

The SPLS series is an informal meeting of the Scottish Programming Languages Institute (SPLI) Community for discussing anything related to programming languages.

This edition is held and organised by the the School of Computing Science at the University of Glasgow.
